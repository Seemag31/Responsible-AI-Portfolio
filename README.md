# Responsible AI Portfolio

This portfolio demonstrates applied Responsible AI practices aligned with industry standards for ethical and safe AI systems.

---

## 1. Risk Assessment
Identifying and documenting potential harms, misuse risks, and impact severity.

## 2. Fairness & Bias Evaluation
Measuring disparities across demographic or user groups and testing mitigation strategies.

## 3. Model Integrity & Robustness
Evaluating reliability, stability, and vulnerability to adversarial inputs.

## 4. Red Teaming
Simulating misuse, stress testing edge cases, and uncovering failure modes.

## 5. Monitoring & Post-Deployment Oversight
Designing metrics, alerts, and feedback loops for real-world model performance.

## 6. Governance & Documentation
Model cards, risk registers, decision logs, and compliance artifacts.
